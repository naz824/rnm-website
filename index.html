Experience Zones

Artisan Village

Shop curated handmade goods, fashion, wellness, and specialty products from local Richmond entrepreneurs.

Creative Kids Hangout

Interactive youth-centered zone with art stations, movement activities, and family-friendly creative learning.

Main Stage

Experience live bands, DJs, dance showcases, and host-led crowd moments throughout the evening.

MakerSpace

Join guided creative projects and rotating installations developed by local visual artists and educators.
