Vendor Information

Richmond Night Market welcomes vendors across handmade goods, prepared food, wellness, apparel, art, and service experiences that align with our community values.

Who Should Apply

  • Independent makers and product-based businesses
  • Food creators with valid permits
  • Artists, designers, and curated community merchants
  • Brand activations aligned with RNM culture

How Selection Works

  • Applications are reviewed for quality, fit, and category balance
  • Accepted vendors receive logistics packet and setup instructions
  • Returning vendors are encouraged to rotate product storytelling
  • All vendors must follow event-day compliance guidelines

Apply for 2026

Submit your business profile, product images, social channels, and preferred months. The vendor team provides confirmations and next steps by email.
