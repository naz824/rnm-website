The Richmond Night Market Experience

Every second Saturday, 17th Street Market transforms into a dynamic environment designed for shopping, live creativity, and neighborhood connection from 5 PM to 9 PM.

Artisan Village

Explore handmade jewelry, home décor, skincare, books, and apparel from Black-owned and community-rooted small businesses.

  • 70+ artisan and retail booths monthly
  • Rotating featured maker showcases
  • Support independent entrepreneurship

Creative Kids Hangout

Kid-focused enrichment with games, crafts, and interactive stations guided by local artists, youth mentors, and volunteers.

  • Hands-on activity tables
  • Family rest area with seating
  • Free take-home mini projects

Main Stage

Live performances showcase Richmond musicians, spoken word artists, dance groups, and DJ sets throughout the evening.

  • Scheduled emcee-hosted blocks
  • Community dance breaks
  • Local artist spotlight segments

MakerSpace

Live art activations and collaborative projects where guests create with professional artists and educators.

  • Rotating monthly themes
  • Community mural opportunities
  • Interactive installations

Accessibility at Every Zone

RNM prioritizes clear walkways, family-friendly flow, ADA access routes, and inclusive language across all wayfinding and digital channels.

Need support on event day? Visit the guest services tent for mobility guidance, volunteer assistance, and sensory break recommendations.
